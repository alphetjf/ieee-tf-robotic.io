Scope & Mission

  • Promote new intelligence generated through robotic adaptation, learning and cognitive development
  • Key research directions:
    • Breakthroughs in human-robot collaboration technologies
    • Intelligent algorithms (fuzzy computing, neural networks, evolutionary computation)
    • Integration of IT, network technologies and robotics
    • Structured processing of information and knowledge

2025 Planned Activities

  • IEEE Symposium on Robotic Intelligence in Informationally Structured Space (RiiSS 2025)
    • Part of SSCI 2025
  • Technical committee meetings
  • International research collaboration initiatives
  • Industry-academic exchange programs
